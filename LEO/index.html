Today isn’t just a celebration of your birth. It’s a reminder of the day the world became better — because you were born into it.

I will always choose you.
In every lifetime, in every version of myself, in every possible universe — it will always be you.

So on your special day, I just want to say thank you.
For being born.
For existing.
For finding your way to me.

You are the most precious part of my life. And I will spend the rest of my days making sure you never feel anything less than deeply, completely loved.

Happy birthday, my heart.
I love you, endlessly. Always.
